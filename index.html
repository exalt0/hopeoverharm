The Mission

Hope Over Harm is dedicated to combating the opioid crisis through a comprehensive approach rooted in Research, Response, and Recovery. We conduct action-driven research to uncover effective, data-informed solutions; provide rapid-response initiatives like opioid awareness and Narcan training; and support long-term recovery by empowering individuals and communities. Having made a significant impact in Appalachia, we are committed to expanding our reach to other underserved areas, always with the goal of saving lives and restoring hope.

Our Sponsors

Virginia Department of Health


The Virginia Department of Health has partnered with Hope Over Harm to support opioid overdose prevention efforts. They have committed to supplying Narcan kits for distribution and have offered 50 training slots through VDH, along with providing a certified trainer to conduct in-person sessions on college campuses.

University of Virginia

The University of Virginia has partnered with Hope Over Harm to expand access to overdose prevention resources on Grounds. UVA has approved the placement of Narcan kits throughout residence halls and is supporting the hosting of REVIVE! training sessions led by the Virginia Department of Health in Charlottesville.
